Summary Of India Vs South Africa ODI Series

The ODI series of India and South Africa was more of a skirmish rather than a battle. AB De Villiers, Hashim Amla, Dale Steyn and the whole South African team literally demolished the Indian Team's bowling attack and batting order. The big names in the SA team were the highlight of the series giving the Indian fans something to enjoy while watching the contests. All in all, India should not look back on this series and focus more on the upcoming Test series.

  • Football Player of the Week: Philippe Coutinho

    Philippe Coutinho, the Liverpool striker proved his worth after he scored the winner against Chelsea on Saturday. The goal was a mixture of skill and presence of mind. He knew where the goalkeeper was standing and placed the ball in the gap between the keeper and the post. This is why he is the football player of the week.

  • Cricket Player of the Week:AB De Villiers

    AB De Villiers, South African Batsman played a great innings of calmness and composure. He destroyed the Indian attack yet again which is why he is the Cricket player of the week.
